Gwilym Llywelyn

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Gwilym Llywelyn (b.1991) is a Welsh documentary photographer based in the UK. He holds a BA in Fine Art Photography from the University of Gloucestershire (2014) and an MA in Photography at the University of West England (2021-2022). The themes of his work explore the effects of historical imperialism and colonialism on modern communities and landscapes.

Exhibitions

2022 - Showcase, The Arnolfini Gallery, Bristol

2014 - Exhibition One, Retina Photo, Centre Space Gallery, Bristol
2014 - ESPY Photography Award, Elysium Gallery, Swansea
2013 - Colony, Fforest, Cardigan
2013 - Kingdom, Beechwood Shopping Centre, Cheltenham
