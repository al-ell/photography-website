Discovery is a work in progress, exploring the route of 19th century American explorers Lewis & Clark and the Corps of Discovery, who were the first recorded people to cross the North American continent from the Mississippi River to the Pacific Ocean. This expedition, commissioned by then president Thomas Jefferson following the Louisiana Purchase, passed entirely through what was then entirely unexplored lands in the eyes of the young United States, being the lone homeland for generations to Indigenous Americans; the route passes now passes through ten US States. Through endless plains and mall parking lots, virgin forests and crowded bars, this little travelled route through the United States set in motion the cogs and wheels in the minds of its citizens that would turn their gaze forever westward, and their nation into what it is today. The Fur Trappers, the Oregon Trail, the Wild West, the Gold Rush, the Reservation System, the Railroad, Manifest Destiny, Murder, Genocide, the American Dream, this is where it all stems from, this is where a nation was created, a land tamed but untamed, united but divided.
